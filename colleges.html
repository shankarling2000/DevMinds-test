We also provide on-request custom services.

Domain Specific Training

1. Customizable Course Structure: DevMinds offers domain-specific training where you can request a course tailored to your interests. Choose the course duration, ranging from a few days to several weeks, based on your learning goals.

2. Online and Offline Modes Available: You can opt for either online or offline training formats, giving you the flexibility to learn in a way that suits your schedule and preferences.

3. Flexible Subject and Domain Selection: Pick the domain and subject of your choice, whether it's tech-based, soft skills, or any other area, ensuring a personalized and targeted learning experience.

Events

1. Tailored Event Formats: We offer customized events like Hackathons, Ideations, Coding Fests, and Carnivals, where you can choose the duration, themes, and challenges based on your objectives.

2. Customizable Focus Areas: Select the event's focus, whether it’s innovation, problem-solving, or skill development in specific domains, ensuring participants engage in meaningful and relevant experiences.

3. Event Delivery: These events can be organized both online or offline, giving you the flexibility to choose the mode that best suits your audience and goals.

Placement Focused Training

1. Placement-Focused Curriculum: We offer Soft Skills and Technical Training programs that are designed to enhance employability by offering practical, job-oriented learning experiences tailored to industry needs.

2. Comprehensive Skill Development: Colleges can choose between soft skills training (aptitude communication, leadership, etc.) or technical training (coding, engineering, etc.), with a focus on bridging gaps for successful placements.

3. Customized Learning: Training sessions are customizable in terms of duration, content, and delivery mode (online/offline), ensuring students receive focused, placement-oriented guidance.
