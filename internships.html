Internship Roadmap

Post enrollment this is how your internship journey will look like :)

Training Enrollment

Candidates enroll for specialized training programs led by expert mentors, aimed at providing the skills needed to secure an Industry Internship post-training.

Project Implementation

Post-training, you'll work on a mini and a major project to apply your skills. Your performance will be assessed based on applied skills, critical thinking, and real-world relevance.

Interns Deployment

Qualified interns are guided by expert mentors and deployed to associated startups or organizations. The internship may be paid or unpaid, depending on the company.
